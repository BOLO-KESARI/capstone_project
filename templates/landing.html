Platform Features

A comprehensive revenue management ecosystem built for modern airlines

Live Aviation Data

Integration with AviationStack API for real-time flight tracking, airport data, airline information, and schedule monitoring.

ML Demand Forecasting

Multi-horizon forecasting using XGBoost, Random Forest, and ARIMA models with SHAP explainability and drift detection.

Dynamic Pricing

Real-time price optimization using elasticity modeling, competitor analysis, and multi-objective Pareto optimization.

Risk Management

Operational risk scoring, overbooking strategy optimization, and regulatory compliance with full audit trail logging.

3-Tier Database

RDBMS (SQLite), OLAP aggregations, and NoSQL passenger intelligence for comprehensive data storage and querying.
